In a perfect world, I'd want the ability to:

- Catalog all of my ingredients that I have at the house. Being able to view them on my
phone, or on a tablet in the kitchen.
- See or search for recipes using the ingredients on-hand, in my phone or on the tablet in
the kitchen
- See or search for recipes using the ingredients on-hand, showing me additional
ingredients that would be needed to make a recipe. Relevant substitutions are helpful as
well, using ingredients I already have
- Easily order ingredients I'm missing from the phone, tablet, or via google home/alexa
  - Hey Alexa, restock my essentials
    - Essentials could be a keyword for a defined list of staples you have in
your kitchen(i.e onions, chicken stock, canned goods(pinto beans, cento
tomatoes, etc)
  - Have the ability to confirm what you're ordering(i.e the only essentials missing
are chicken stock, onions, russet potatoes, and cento tomatoes
    - Get updates on when the ingredients are ready for pickup.
      - "I checked your pantry and see you are missing chicken stock,
onions, russet potatoes, and cento tomatoes. Would you like me
to order these from Target?"
      - "Great, they will be ready for pickup in 2 hours. I'll notify you when
they're ready for pickup" - Target)
- Be able to check-in items to your pantry easily when you buy them, either from the store
physically or online. I do not want to add them manually.
  - Near-field RFID?
  - Integrations w/locations people shop at?(i.e fetching shopping history from API -
i.e QFC rewards card login information)
  - Photo recognition of multiple items sounds cool, but I'm worried about accuracy,
and it's an extra step.
- Easily subtract ingredients when I've used them
  - When I've signaled intent to use them for a recipe from the app
  - When I've used them for a recipe I've used without the app, and just want to keep
my pantry up to date
    - EVERYTHING GOES BACK TO THE PANTRY + THE TOOLS I HAVE
- Ingredients($$, availability, accessibility, quality, season) + tools +
time
  - The Pantry is the core to any dish
- Cost of the items I have in my pantry, what i've spent on groceries this month
  - After a few months(or after a few hundred users) finding my patterns & eventually
projecting how much I will spend on groceries based on my usage
- Meal planning mode
  - Creating recipe plan on sunday afternoons, with all the ingredients I have, as well
as my caloric specifications(protein, vitamins, no carbs etc)
- Weight loss mode
  - Integrate with fitbit, apple health, etc to track caloric intake for the meals I make,
based on the ingredients used(the recipes)
- Digitize physical recipes
